about me

Nextag was a comparison shopping website that allowed consumers to compare prices and products across various retailers. It was founded in 1999 by Geehan Chang, and it quickly became one of the most popular comparison shopping engines on the internet. Nextag's platform allowed users to search for a specific product and see prices from different online retailers, enabling them to find the best deals. However, in recent years, the popularity of comparison shopping websites has declined due to changes in online shopping behavior, such as the rise of e-commerce giants like Amazon and the increasing use of mobile shopping apps. As a result, Nextag faced stiff competition and struggled to maintain its relevance in the market.
